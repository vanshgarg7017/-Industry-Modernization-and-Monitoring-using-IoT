# Industry Modernization and Monitoring Circuit Connections

- VCC: 5V
- GND: GND

## Ultrasonic Sensor

- ECHO: D19

- GND: GND

## MQ2 Gas Sensor
- DO: D34
- AO: D2

## PIR Motion Sensor
